# Blog Image Guide: Tactile Editorial Aesthetic

This guide defines the visual standard for all blog post header images on the Canadian Optimizer website. To maintain a premium, trustworthy, and consistent brand, follow these stylistic rules.

## 1. Core Visual Pillar: "High-Fidelity Realism"
We avoid abstract 3D renders, glowing icons, or generic clip art. Every image must look like a high-end editorial photograph from a publication like *The Economist* or *The Wall Street Journal*.

- **Natural Lighting**: Use golden hour or soft window light. Avoid harsh shadows or clinical studio lighting.
- **Shallow Depth of Field**: Use bokeh to focus on tactile details (e.g., the texture of a leather binder or the engraving on a fountain pen).
- **Quality**: Photorealistic, 8k resolution, 35mm or 50mm lens feel.

## 2. Topic Relevance & Props
Each image must be directly relevant to the article's subject matter.

- **Finance/Technical Topics**: High-end leather planners, scientific calculators, silver fountain pens, crisp financial documents.
- **Lifestyle/Retirement Topics**: Serene Canadian landscapes (Rockies, Muskoka, Vancouver Coast), high-quality outdoor furniture, luxury luggage tags.
- **Institutional/Corporate Topics**: Modern Toronto/Vancouver architecture (glass, steel), boardroom mahogany tables, leather briefcases.

## 3. Strict Identity Constraints
> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Canadian Currency Only**: If money is shown, it **MUST** be Canadian currency (polymer 20, 50, or 100 dollar bills with clear holographic features, or Loonies/Toonies).
> **NO AMERICAN MONEY**: Never include green paper bills or US coinage.
> **No Text on Images**: Avoid baked-in titles or labels unless they are part of a realistic object (e.g., a "Final Will" header on a document).

## 4. Master Prompt Formula
Use this formula for consistent results with AI generation tools:

> "High-end editorial photography of [RELEVANT_SUBJECT]. Style: Tactile realism, professional financial magazine aesthetic. Lighting: [SOFT_WINDOW_LIGHT/GOLDEN_HOUR]. Props: [SPECIFIC_CANADIAN_ELEMENTS]. Details: Shallow depth of field, bokeh, 8k resolution, photorealistic. NO TEXT. NO AMERICAN CURRENCY."

## 5. Directory & Performance
- **Path**: `public/images/blog/[slug].png`
- **Format**: Optimized PNG or WebP.
- **Aspect Ratio**: 1:1 or 16:9 (standardized to 1024x1024 typically for the current design).
